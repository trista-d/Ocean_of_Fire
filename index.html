How to Play



You are participating in the most prestigious horse race ever known to exist: the Ocean of Fire. This race will take place over the span of three days, each becoming more difficult to succeed. Avoid prickly cacti, rough bramble, and poisonous scorpions, as these can cause your horse harm.

Use the arrow keys to avoid obstacles. Hitting an obstacle will result in you losing a life. If you lose three lives your horse will be incapacitated and therefore eliminated from the race!
